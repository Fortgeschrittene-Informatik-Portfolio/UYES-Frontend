UYES! Rules >
  • Each player gets 7 cards.
  • The rest goes into a draw pile; one card is turned over to start the discard pile.
  • You can play a card if it matches the same color or number.
  • Special cards:
    • +2: Next player draws 2 cards and skips a turn
    • Skip: Next player is skipped
    • Reverse: Reverses the direction of play
    • Wild: Player chooses the next color
    • +4 Wild: Like Wild, but next player draws 4 cards
  • Say "UYES!" when you only have 1 card left!
  • The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
